Some of my attempts of sketching and drawing

By: Dennis Núñez, December 2020.


Pattern of tree roots, tree branches, which are similar to lightning, neurons, river courses, and more. Fractals is a mathematical attempt to describe these interesting structures.


This drawing of trees symbolizes the similarity between the different fields of science and humanities. Each tree represents a field and the branches represent each subfield. Occasionally they cross each other.


Drawing of the photo of the Earth, taken by Voyager 1. Everything you know and all of human history is in that pale blue dot, in an atomic part of the universe.
