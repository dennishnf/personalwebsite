About me

I obtained my M.Sc. degree in Digital Sciences with focus in AI at the Université Paris Cité (France), ranked second in the graduating class. Previously, I obtained my B.Sc. degree in Electronic Engineering at the Universidad Nacional de Ingeniería (UNI), Peru, ranked first in the graduating class. My research experience in Deep Learning, Computer Vision and NLP includes my internships at AGH University of Science and Technology (Poland), University of Padova (Italy), Université Sorbonne Paris Nord (France), DiliTrust (France), and Nokia Bell Labs (France). I have presented several of my research projects in workshops at major AI conferences, including NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR and CVPR. Additionally, I have served as a paper reviewer for NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML and AAAI conferences on multiple occasions. To further broaden my expertise, I've participated in prestigious AI and ML summer schools. Beyond academia and research, my interests cover a wide range of areas, such as cybersecurity, mathematics, physics, and philosophy.
